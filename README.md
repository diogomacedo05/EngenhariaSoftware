🏠 IMOIA Smart — The Future of Real Estate
Software Engineering Project | UTAD 2024/2025

IMOIA Smart is a cutting-edge digital platform designed to modernize the real estate market. By integrating Artificial Intelligence (AI), the system optimizes property promotion, provides personalized financial simulations, and streamlines the complex process of buying, selling, or renting properties.

🌟 Key Features
🤖 AI-Powered Property Valuation: Automatic initial assessment of legal and commercial data before expert review.

📉 Smart Financing Simulator: Tailored simulations focusing on young buyers (18-35), including IMT/IS tax exemptions and public guarantees.

🔍 Advanced Search: High-precision filters for location, price, and eligibility for government incentives.

📅 Automated Management: Smart scheduling for visits and detailed tracking of negotiation conditions.

💬 AI Support: 24/7 Chatbot and FAQ system to assist users throughout their journey.

🛠 Project Lifecycle
The development was divided into four strategic stages to ensure a robust software engineering process:


📍 Phase 1: Requirements & Analysis
User Stories: Defining the "Who, What, and Why" for every user type.

Functional Requirements: Mapping out 29 specific system functionalities.

Use Case Modeling: Detailing interactions between actors (Users, Admins, Promoters) and the system.


🏗 Phase 2: Structural Design
Class Diagrams: Defining the static structure, attributes, and methods of the system (Users, Properties, Financing, etc.).

Object Diagrams: Real-world instances and scenarios to validate the data model.


⚡ Phase 3: Dynamic Modeling
Activity Diagrams: Visualizing complex workflows like property registration and registration flows.

State Diagrams: Tracking the lifecycle of critical objects, such as property approval states.


🎨 Phase 4: Prototyping
High-Fidelity Mockups: A complete non-functional prototype showcasing the UI/UX for desktop and mobile.

Interactive Design: Demonstrating the AI chatbot interface and smart dashboards.
